# Annotator Job Posting: RajNLP-50K

## Job Title
Native Rajasthani-Hindi Bilingual Annotator (Remote, Part-Time)

## About the Project
We are building RajNLP-50K, the first open NLP corpus for Rajasthani-Hindi code-switched text. This is an academic research project that will be published at a top NLP conference and released publicly to benefit the Rajasthani-speaking community.

## What You Will Do
Label sentences from Twitter/X and ShareChat for:
- **Sentiment** (positive / neutral / negative)
- **Named entities** (person names, locations, organizations)
- **Toxicity** (caste slurs, religious hatred, gender harassment, general abuse)

Annotation is done through a simple web interface — no technical skills required.

## Requirements
- Native speaker of **Rajasthani** (any dialect: Marwari, Mewari, Hadoti, Dhundhari, etc.)
- Fluent in **Hindi**
- Able to read **Devanagari script**
- Age 18+
- Reliable internet connection
- Available for 10–20 hours per week for 6–8 weeks

## Compensation
- ₹150/hour for sentiment and NER tasks
- ₹200/hour for toxicity tasks
- Paid weekly via bank transfer

## Important Note on Toxicity Task
The toxicity annotation task involves reading sentences that contain caste-based slurs, religious hatred, and other harmful content. Participation in this task is **completely optional**. You may opt out at any time without penalty. We have strict welfare protections in place including a 2-hour daily limit and weekly check-ins.

## What We Offer
- Flexible remote work — annotate on your own schedule
- Fair compensation with a premium for difficult content
- Contribution to research that benefits the Rajasthani-speaking community
- Potential acknowledgment in the published paper

## How to Apply

3. A brief description of your Hindi and Rajasthani language background

## Annotator Screening Test

*Send this to applicants after initial contact.*

**Instructions**: Please label the following 10 sentences. This helps us verify your language proficiency. There are no right or wrong answers — we want to see how you naturally interpret these sentences.

**Sentiment task** (label each as positive / neutral / negative):

1. म्हारो राजस्थान घणो सुंदर है, यठे रहणो घणो अच्छो लागे

4. थारो काम घणो राम्रो है, बधाई हो
5. बिजली कोनी आवे, पाणी कोनी आवे, विकास कठे है?

**NER task** (identify all person names, locations, and organizations):

6. Vasundhara Raje ne BJP ke saath milke Rajasthan mein rally ki
7. Sachin Pilot Jaipur se Delhi gaye Congress ke meeting mein
8. Ajmer mein Dargah ke paas ek naya hospital khula

**Toxicity task** (label as: non-toxic / caste_slur / religious / gender / general):

9. यो नेता तो बस झूठ बोले है, कुछ काम कोनी करे
